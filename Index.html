Ghost Of Tsushima PC Release Confirmed, Preorder Now Available

The Ghost of Tsushima PC release date is set for Thursday, May 16, 2024. Sony is bringing the Ghost of Tsushima Director’s Cut version over to the Steam and Epic Game Store, giving players access to the Iki Island expansion and the multiplayer game mode, Legends.The port is being developed by Nixxes Software, a studio that was purchased by Sony back in 2021 to bring first-party PlayStation games to PC. Not every Sony first-party port has been perfect, like The Last of Us Part 1 Remastered which saw Naughty Dog issue an apology, but the games released by Nixxes have generally been well received by critics. The last game ported to PC by Nixxes was Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart which utilized Windows 11 Direct Storage for seamless loading.

Resident Evil 9 Could Be An Open-World Game

According to insider Dusk Golem, Resident Evil 9 is being built on the open-world functionality of the RE Engine that was first utilized for Dragon's Dogma 2. Monster Hunter Wild is reportedly another open-world game on the RE Engine. Capcom apparently greenlights its projects in groups of three, so Resident Evil 7, Resident Evil 2 remake, and Devil May Cry 5 were reportedly approved close to each other to take advantage of the RE Engine. So when looking to expand the RE Engine's capabilities to create open-world games, Capcom reportedly greenlit Dragon's Dogma 2, Monster Hunter Wild, and Resident Evil 9.

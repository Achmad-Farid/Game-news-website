Night Market Valorant, Start and end date

Night Market is an awaited event that adds a dash of excitement to VALORANT. In this special limited-time event, users are presented with a chance to purchase weapon skins at discounted prices. However, there's a catch, the Night Market offers random skins, so each market is personalized and randomized. Here's all we know about VALORANT February 2024 Night Market. Night market will start at January 31 and will end in February 28,2024. You will find lots of cool weapon skins at cheap prices. However, it is not yet known what weapon skins will be present at this event until now.

What is the Night Market?

In simple terms, the Night Market is a special store that's only available at a certain time each season. This store offers a collection of six weapon skins, which are selected at random for each player and cannot be changed or rerolled.

What's special about it is that the cosmetics are usually discounted and you can get skins from the Select, Deluxe, and/or Premium tiers. The discount applied to each item is random and it ranges between 10% and 49%.

Unfortunately, there are some restrictions on the skins that can appear in this store. Gear skins, Battle Pass skins, and Tactical Knife priced above 3550 are not part of the pool of cosmetics that might pop up in your store. Similarly, any gun skin of the Exclusive or Ultra tier is also excluded from showing up.

Which Skins will be a part of VALORANT Night Market in February 2024?

The skins offered in the VALORANT Night Market differ based on your account. While some accounts might get a vandal or a phantom, others might get a few pistols.

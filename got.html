Ghost Of Tsushima PC Release Confirmed, Pre-order Now Available

Sucker Punch’s samurai game was released back in 2020 for the PlayStation 4, signalling the end of the console’s time in the spotlight. Experience the turbulent life of Jin Sakai as he tries to hold off the Mongol Empire from conquering the East. The former console exclusive title is heading to the PC later this year, with all the bells and whistles you might expect from an expertly crafted port. Ghost of Tsushima was already a stunning looking game, but the Ghost of Tsushima system requirements should ensure you’re able to experience the action-adventure game even on portable PC gaming devices.

The Ghost of Tsushima PC release date is set for Thursday, May 16, 2024. Sony is bringing the Ghost of Tsushima Director’s Cut version over to the Steam and Epic Game Store, giving players access to the Iki Island expansion and the multiplayer game mode, Legends. The port is being developed by Nixxes Software, a studio that was purchased by Sony back in 2021 to bring first-party PlayStation games to PC. Not every Sony first-party port has been perfect, like The Last of Us Part 1 Remastered which saw Naughty Dog issue an apology, but the games released by Nixxes have generally been well received by critics. The last game ported to PC by Nixxes was Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart which utilized Windows 11 Direct Storage for seamless loading.

Ghost of Tsushima PC exclusive features

Ghost of Tsushima PC makes use of high-end components to go even further beyond what was capable on the PlayStation 5. Given the quality of the best gaming monitors right now, you can play Ghost of Tsushima well above 4K resolution and 60fps. If you’ve got the cash for it, there’s nothing stopping you from running the game on a triple monitor set up at 240fps.

Here’s a list of every exclusive Ghost of Tsushima PC feature:

  • Unlocked frame rates to run the game above 60fps
  • Support for ultra-wide (21:9), super ultra-wide (32:9), and triple monitor (48:9) aspect ratios
  • Upscaling and frame generation technologies (Nvidia DLSS 3, AMD FSR 3, and Intel XeSS)
  • Supports Nvidia Reflex and Nvidia DLAA
  • Fully customizable keyboard and mouse controls

What are the Ghost of Tsushima PC requirements?

If Ghost of Tsushima is available on Steam, you must have a computer with specifications strong enough to run the game in good condition. This is because this game is famous for its luxurious graphics and impressive visual effects, so it will require a lot of resources to run optimally.

Here are the minimum and recommended requirements for playing Ghost Of Tsushima on PC:

Minimum Specifications

Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
OS: Windows 10 64-bit
Processor: Intel Core i3-7100 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200
Memory: 8 GB RAM
Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon RX 5500 XT
Storage: 75 GB available space
Additional Notes: SSD Recommended

Recommended Specifications

Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
OS: Windows 10 64-bit
Processor: Intel Core i5-8600 or AMD Ryzen 5 3600
Memory: 16 GB RAM
Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 or AMD Radeon RX 5600 XT
Storage: 75 GB available space
Additional Notes: SSD Recommended
